Winter Clothing

Winter clothing are clothes used for protection against the particularly cold weather of winter.[1 ] Often they have a good water resistance, consist of multiple layers to protect and insulate against low temperatures.[2]

Churidar

Churidars, or more properly churidar pyjamas, are tightly fitting trousers worn by both men and women in the Indian subcontinent. Churidars are a variant of the common salwar pants. Salwars are cut wide at the top and narrow at the ankle.
