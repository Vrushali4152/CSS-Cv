The traditional attire of the Assamese people is Dhoti-Kurta for men and Mekhla- Chadar for the womenfolk. Odisha is famous for Kataki Sari and Sambalpuri Sari. These sari designs contain very peculiar designs and seem very dignified Most of the women in Odisha interested in wearing these saris during festival occasions, Marriage occasions and other auspicious days.
